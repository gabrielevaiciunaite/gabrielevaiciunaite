### 📊 GameZone E-Commerce Performance Analysis

Analysis of e-commerce performance across sales, customers, products, channels, and regions.

- Analyzed **$6.2M GMV** across **22K orders** and **20K customers**
- Identified strong revenue concentration, with the **top 3 products generating $5.20M (~84%)**
- Detected low customer retention, with only **9.35% repeat purchase rate**
- Highlighted channel imbalance, with **Direct traffic contributing ~$5.21M (~84% of revenue)**
- Identified **North America** as the strongest region, generating **$3.21M (~52% of total revenue)**

**Focus:** business performance analysis, customer behavior, retention, channel performance, regional trends
